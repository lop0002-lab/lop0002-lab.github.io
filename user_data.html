Japan's low score likely reflects active national campaigns against addiction and a cultural norm of disciplined digital usage, which less-connected nations have yet to adopt.

Nations newer to widespread social media adoption — particularly in South Asia — show mid-to-high scores, likely because cultural and legislative guardrails haven't yet caught up to platform penetration.
