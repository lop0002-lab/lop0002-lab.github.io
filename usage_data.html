Examining how students interact with social media — how often, for what reasons, and at what cost to their sleep and mental health.

01 — Behaviour

Purposeless Scrolling

Most users scroll social media with no specific goal. Only 18% said they rarely or never do so — the majority scroll often or always.

This pattern was most pronounced among high school and university students — those with the least structured daily routine showed the highest rates of purposeless usage.
